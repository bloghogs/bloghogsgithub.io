Top Selling Laptops in 2023!

Take a deeper look into Amazons list the top selling smartphones in 2023 so far, and why they are the top sellers.

A combination of factors such as brand reputation, price, features and specifications, reviews and ratings, marketing and promotion and availability contribute to making a smartphone a best seller on Amazon. Popular and well-established brands, reasonably priced smartphones with advanced features and positive reviews tend to be more popular among consumers.

1. Samsung Galaxy A53 5G

The Samsung Galaxy A53 5G is a mid-range smartphone that was released in 2021. It features a 6.5-inch Super AMOLED display, a octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 750G processor, up to 6GB of RAM, and up to 128GB of internal storage. It runs on Android 11 with One UI 3.1. It has a quad-camera setup on the back, a 64-megapixel main camera, an ultra-wide-angle camera, a macro camera and a depth sensor. The front-facing camera is 32 megapixel. It also has 5G connectivity, a 4500mAh battery with 15W fast charging and a in-display fingerprint scanner. It also has a 3.5mm headphone jack and a USB-C port. It is available in various colors.
It is a great choice for those who are looking for a affordable 5G phone with good camera and good performance.

2. Samsung Galaxy A03s

The Samsung Galaxy A03s is an entry-level smartphone that was launched in 2021. It has a 6.5 inches IPS LCD display with a resolution of 720 x 1600 pixels. It is powered by a Octa-core processor with 2GB or 3GB of RAM and 32GB or 64GB of internal storage which can be expanded up to 1TB via a microSD card. It runs on Android 11 with One UI 3.1. The device has a triple camera setup on the back, which includes a 13-megapixel main sensor, a 2-megapixel macro lens, and a 2-megapixel depth sensor. The front-facing camera is 5 megapixel. It has a 5000mAh battery with 15W fast charging support and a rear-mounted fingerprint scanner. It also has a 3.5mm headphone jack and a micro USB port for charging and data transfer. Available in various colors.
It is a budget friendly device for those who are looking for a basic smartphone with decent camera and battery life.

3. Samsung Galaxy A03 Core

The Samsung Galaxy A03 Core is an entry-level smartphone that was launched in 2021. It has a 5.3 inches PLS TFT display with a resolution of 480 x 960 pixels. It runs on Android 11 (Go edition) which is a lightweight version of Android optimized for devices with less powerful hardware. The device has a dual camera setup on the back, which includes a 5-megapixel main sensor and a 2-megapixel depth sensor. The front-facing camera is 5 megapixel. It has a 3000mAh battery and a rear-mounted fingerprint scanner. It has a micro USB port for charging and data transfer. Available in various colors.
It is a budget friendly device for those who are looking for a basic smartphone with basic features.

5. Google Pixel 6a

The Pixel 6a is a smartphone with a 6.2-inch display, 4GB or 6GB of RAM and a chip designed by Google specifically for Pixel, called Tensor, which makes it smart, secure and powerful. It has an adaptive battery that can last over 24 hours and learns your favorite apps to conserve power on the ones you rarely use. With the Extreme Battery Saver feature, the battery can last up to 72 hours. With Live Translate, you can chat in 11 languages, interpret face-to-face conversations and translate signs with a camera. There's also a Magic Eraser feature in Google Photos which allows you to remove distractions or change the color and brightness so objects blend in.
The Pixel 6a is good option for budget-conscious consumers who are looking for a smartphone that offers good performance, camera capabilities and battery life.

6. Apple iPhone SE 2020

The Apple iPhone SE (2020) is a budget-friendly smartphone that was released in April 2020. It has a 4.7-inch Retina IPS LCD display with a resolution of 750 x 1334 pixels. It is powered by the A13 Bionic chip, which is the same chip used in the iPhone 11, and has 3GB of RAM. It comes in three storage options: 64GB, 128GB, and 256GB. It runs on iOS 13 and is upgradable to iOS 14. The device has a single 12-megapixel camera on the back and a 7-megapixel front-facing camera. It has a 1821 mAh battery and supports fast charging with an 18W adapter and wireless charging. It also has Touch ID fingerprint sensor built into the home button. It's available in various colors. It is a budget-friendly option for those looking for an iPhone with a smaller form factor and a budget-friendly price point.
This phone is a good choice for those who want an iPhone with a compact design and powerful performance, but don't want to spend a lot of money. It is worth noting that some specs and features may vary depending on the region or carrier.

The iPhone XR is a good choice for those who want an iPhone with a large screen, good camera and powerful performance, but don't want to spend as much as the more expensive iPhone models.
